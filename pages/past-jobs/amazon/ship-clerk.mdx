# T3 Shipdock Process Assistant

## Summary

> As a Shipping Clerk, Paul was responsible for overseeing the dock operations. This role requires **strong leadership** to manage personnel and labor hours, exceptional coordination skills to liaise between dock employees, yard jockeys, and shipping partners, and a **rigorous approach** to ensure accurate and timely shipments.

### Location

- Mechanicsburg, PA
- Amazon Fulfillment Center - PHL4

## Key Responsibilities

### Leadership 

- Supervise and lead a team of dock employees, providing guidance, training, and support.
- Plan and allocate labor hours and personnel to meet shipping demands efficiently.
- Foster a positive work environment that encourages teamwork and accountability.

### Coordination

- Serve as the primary contact between dock employees, yard jockeys, and shipping partners/drivers.
- Coordinate loading and unloading activities to ensure timely departures and arrivals.
- Communicate effectively with all stakeholders to resolve issues and enhance operations.

### Accuracy and Rigor 

- Ensure all packages and trucks are accurately prepared for their correct destinations.
- Implement rigorous checks and procedures to prevent errors and minimize costs.
- Conduct regular audits to verify shipment accuracy and compliance with policies.

## Skill Development

- Proven experience in a shipping or logistics role, with years in a supervisory capacity.
- Strong leadership skills with the ability to motivate and manage a diverse team.
- Excellent organizational and time-management skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency in shipping software and Microsoft Office Suite.
- Knowledge of safety regulations and best practices in a shipping environment.
