# Billing Data Analyst

## Summary

As a Billing Data Analyst, Paul was responsible for managing and analyzing the organization's billing processes to ensure accuracy and efficiency. This role involved working closely with finance, sales, and customer service departments to maintain seamless billing operations. The Billing Data Analyst played a crucial role in analyzing billing data, resolving discrepancies, and suggesting improvements to enhance billing procedures.

### Company Details

- **Location:** Hummelstown, PA
- **Duration:** '22 - current
- **Company:** [Flagger Force](/flagger-force)

## Key Responsibilities

### Data Analysis and Reporting

- Analyze billing data to identify trends, issues, and opportunities for improvement.
- Prepare regular billing reports for management review.
- Create analytical reports and present findings to senior managers.

### Invoice Management

- Generate and issue accurate invoices in a timely manner.
- Review invoices to ensure billing accuracy.
- Monitor accounts receivable and follow up on overdue payments.

### Process Improvement

- Identify opportunities to enhance billing procedures.
- Suggest and implement improvements to the invoicing process.

### Discrepancy Resolution

- Investigate and resolve billing discrepancies with customers and internal departments.
- Address customer complaints and questions related to billing.

### Compliance and Auditing

- Ensure billing processes comply with company policies and regulatory requirements.
- Support monthly and quarterly audits.

## Skill Development

- Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
- 2-3 years of experience in billing, finance, or a similar role.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, especially Excel.
- Experience with accounting software and databases (e.g., QuickBooks, SAP).
- Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills.
- Effective communication and interpersonal skills.
